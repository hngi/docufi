Compare Documents
You now have the ability to compare two documents that can
be in different formats and see the highlighted difference in
the results. Know exactly what has been changed, paraphrased, or
copied between two textual documents. This diff tool allows you
to see the similarity in the files quickly and easily.
  • Compare online content by submitting URLs
  • Compare raw text - simply type in your text in a text box
  • Compare text types: html, txt, pdf, doc, rtf, xml, ppt, odt, chm, epub, odp
  • Compare images with textual content using OCR: jpg, jpeg, png, bmp, gif
  • Upload files from these platforms: Dropbox, Google Drive, One Drive
  • Compare your files and the URLs for free
